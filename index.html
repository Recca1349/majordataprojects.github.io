Café Harmony Sales Performance Analysis

This project focused on helping Café Harmony, a fast-growing virtual café chain, overcome operational challenges during its expansion phase. Using Excel, I cleaned, analyzed, and visualized datasets covering sales, customer demographics, inventory, employee performance, and customer feedback. Key insights revealed top-selling products, high-value customer segments, frequently understocked items, performance gaps among employees, and low customer satisfaction levels. Based on these findings, strategic recommendations were implemented, resulting in reduced stock shortages, improved staff performance, increased customer ratings, and higher revenue — all within a 3-month post-implementation period. The final deliverable included an interactive Excel dashboard with key KPIs, pivot charts, and slicers to support ongoing decision-making.

Retail sales and customer behavior analysis using Excel dashboards

Frontier Mart, our client, approached us to help them gain clarity on their sales performance and customer behavior across their broad product offerings. Despite being a leading retailer in the consumer goods space, they struggled with understanding monthly sales trends, identifying top-performing products, and analyzing customer payment preferences. Through detailed data analysis using Microsoft Excel, we uncovered several areas needing attention: unclear performance of product categories, inconsistent profit margins, and lack of insight into customer purchase methods. These discoveries enabled us to recommend focused strategies—such as promoting high-performing items, optimizing sales channels, and leveraging preferred payment modes—that empowered Frontier Mart to drive more informed, data-backed decisions and enhance their overall business performance.
